Mashed Potato

Make with potatoes, milk, butter, and optional garlic, this go to recipe for simple, homemade mashed potatoes is a classic holiday dish. Everyone should learn how to make mashed potatoes that come out smooth, creamy, and delicious every time!

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds golden creamer potatoe, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives

Directions:
